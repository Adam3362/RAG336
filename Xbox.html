    Xbox 360 S

    The Xbox 360 S features a similar but refined design to its predecessor. The Xbox 360 S is 17% smaller in form factor than it's predecessor and features significant design changes such as the removal of the two memory card slots featured on previous models as well as relocation of the USB controller receptacle, The initial version included an upgraded 250GB hard drive, with limited editions featuring a larger 320GB model.
